🔧 BUILD INSTRUCTIONS

STEP 1: ASSEMBLE
  ml64.exe RawrXD_TextEditorGUI.asm /c /Fo TextEditorGUI.obj /W3

STEP 2: LINK
  link.exe TextEditorGUI.obj kernel32.lib user32.lib gdi32.lib ^
      /OUT:TextEditorGUI.exe /SUBSYSTEM:WINDOWS /MACHINE:X64

STEP 3: RUN
  TextEditorGUI.exe

REQUIRED LIBRARIES:
  • kernel32.lib (Window APIs, timers, etc)
  • user32.lib (UI, messages, dialogs)
  • gdi32.lib (Graphics, fonts, drawing)
