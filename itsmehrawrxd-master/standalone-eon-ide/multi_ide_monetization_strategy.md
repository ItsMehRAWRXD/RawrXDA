-  **Target**: Regulated, air-gapped, offline-first scenarios

- **Defense contractors** (CMMC, NIST compliance)
- **Financial services** (SOX, air-gapped trading systems)  
- **Healthcare** (HIPAA-compliant development environments)
- **Industrial control** (OT networks, no internet access)
- **Government** (classified systems, SCIF environments)

#### Pricing Model:
```
Enterprise Site License: $50k-150k/year
```

#### Revenue Projection:
- **Year 1**: 10 enterprise customers × $75k average = $750k

- **Ceiling**: $25M ARR (limited by niche market size)
