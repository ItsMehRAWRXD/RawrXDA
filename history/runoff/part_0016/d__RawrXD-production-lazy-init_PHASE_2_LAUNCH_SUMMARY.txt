## Troubleshooting Guide

### Common Issue: Unresolved External Symbol
**Cause**: Function not in scope  
**Solution**: Verify masm_master_include.asm is included

### Common Issue: Compilation Takes Forever
**Cause**: Building all files at once  
**Solution**: Do incremental builds, clean old .obj files

### Common Issue: Performance Overhead >5%
**Cause**: Too much logging  
**Solution**: Reduce logging frequency, use sampling

### Common Issue: Logging Infinite Loop
**Cause**: Logging.asm recursion  
**Solution**: Use conditional compilation, careful review
