## 🎯 **How to Control the Terminal:**

### **Option 1: Toggle (Ctrl+J)**
- Press **`Ctrl+J`** to show/hide terminal
- Toggles between open and closed
- Smooth animation

### **Option 2: Hide Button (NEW!)**
- Click **`⬇️ Hide`** button in terminal header
- Completely minimizes the terminal
- Frees up screen space for chat input!

### **Option 3: Show Again**
- Press **`Ctrl+J`** to bring it back
- Or call `showTerminalPanel()` from console
