### **Core Files:**
- `BigDaddyG-Standalone.html` - Complete IDE with embedded AI
- `BigDaddyG-Desktop.exe` - Portable desktop app
- `BigDaddyG-Core.js` - Embedded AI engine (no external deps)
- `NeuroSymphonic-Engine.js` - Emotional intelligence system

- ✅ **Zero Dependencies** - No Node.js, no Ollama, no servers

- ✅ **Portable** - Single folder, works anywhere
- ✅ **Cross-Platform** - Windows, Mac, Linux (via browser)
